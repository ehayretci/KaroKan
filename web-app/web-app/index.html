karokan

Game Rules

🎯 Objective

  • Be the first to reach 7 points by pushing your opponent’s pieces off the board.

🔄 Turn Structure

  • Place one dot on any empty cell, each round.
  • Then slide every one of your dots by one step in the chosen direction (↖, ↗, ←, →, ↙, ↘).

⚡ Movement

  • If a piece has an empty cell in the chosen direction, it slides there.
  • If there’s no empty space to shift into, pieces remain in place.
  • You can push opponent pieces, but only if your contiguous run is longer.
  • Every piece pushed off the grid earns 1 point.

🕹️ Controls

  • You can use both the arrows and keyboard keys to move your pieces:
  • W: Up-Left (↖)
  • E: Up-Right (↗)
  • A: Left (←)
  • D: Right (→)
  • Z: Down-Left (↙)
  • X: Down-Right (↘)
